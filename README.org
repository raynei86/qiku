* Qiku - A chess library in Common Lisp

** Usage
This is at a functional stage where it passes ~perft~ up to depth 6 (last time I checked).
Exported symbols can be found in ~package.lisp~

*** Creating positions
Still at a barebone stage, FEN strings will come soon.
#+begin_src lisp
(defparameter *test-state* (qiku:make-state)) ;; Initializes to default position
#+end_src

*** Moves and move generation
#+begin_src lisp
(defparameter *test-state* (qiku:make-state))
(let ((moves (qiku:generate-legal-moves *test-state*)))
  (loop
     for m in moves
     do ((do-move! (*test-state* m))
	 (undo-move! (*test-state* m)))))
#+end_src

*** Perft
#+begin_src lisp
(let ((*test-state* (qiku:make-state)))
  (qiku:perft *test-state* 5))
#+end_src
